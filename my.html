PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy policy defines how Datricle team collect and use users personal and non-personal information and data in our mobile applications(available for smart phones and tablets) and on our website.

Personal Data:

Datricle respects your privacy rights and recognize the importance of protecting any information collected about you

Datricle do not collect any personal user data. Datricle may use tools or third-party analytics software to collect and use certain non-personal data that does not enable us to identify you (we track only data such as: device type, mobile device software, data carrier, ...). Datricle uses Google Analytics to analyze anonymous aggregated usage data for our applications.
Non-Personal Data:

Advertisers who serve ads may use technology which can uniquely identify mobile devices to deliver better target messages and control number of times you get an Add. Add Networks may access your unique device identifier through their own technologies and use it to target advertising to you.

Advertisers make available links through advertisements or otherwise enabling you to access third party sites, tools or games. Please note that, while using such sites, tools or games, you are not affiliated with or controlled by us. Datricle applications use Google AdMob ads as primary ad service with included collaboration (ad mediation) with AppBrain and InMobi ad networks.

When users leave feedback on the mobile app we collect the form data, and also the sender’s mobile’s model information. The data is only collected to resolve the errors, bugs or know the demands of the user, so that we can improve the functionalities of the application.
